# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in AI-Agent-Cloud a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
- Harassment and exclusionary language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Threats of violence or abuse
- Disrespectful behavior directed at any individual or group

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue and marking it as a Code of Conduct concern.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reviewed by project maintainers, and may result in temporary or permanent bans from the community.
